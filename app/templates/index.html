📡 Simulation of Real-Time Packet Map 📡

📍 How to Use?

  • 🌍 Each point on the map represents an incoming package.
  • 🟥 Red markers show suspicious activity.
  • 🟩 Green markers show normal activity.
  • 👆 Hover over a point to see IP address and status details.
  • 📊 Check the activity graph for real-time traffic trends.
  • 🔁 Markers fade out after 10 seconds to reduce clutter.
  • ▶️ Use the start/stop buttons to control data simulation.
